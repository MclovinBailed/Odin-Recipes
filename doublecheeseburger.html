Double Cheeseburger

Description

In 1937, Bob's Big Boy is said to have created the first Double Cheese Burger. It would go on to have a bun in the middle of it, so it sort of resembled today's Big Mac from Mcdonald's. Nevertheless, they are still credited for making the first Double Cheeseburger.

    Ingredients
  • Brioche Buns
  • Mayo
  • Pickles
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • American Cheese
  • 2 Burger Patty's
      • Steps
  • Grab your Pattie's season it to your liking, and pop it onto the grill
  • Prepare your Brioche buns by adding mayo to it
  • Place your Pickles
  • Place your Lettuce
  • Place your tomatoes
  • Once the patties are cooked to your liking, place them on the buns
  • Once placed add the American Cheese immediately after to allow it to melt
